The Intelligent Cleaning Engine

The Omnimorpha AI Dishwasher uses advanced cameras, adaptive cleaning modules, and precision-controlled jets to create a fully optimized wash cycle tailored to every dish inside. No two cycles are ever the same.

8-Stage Intelligent Operation

  1. ScanAI identifies dish size, shape, and soil level.
  2. Map3D layout of the rack is generated in real time.
  3. AdaptBrush discs and jets reposition for optimal coverage.
  4. CleanTargeted jets + rotating brushes remove tough debris.
  5. CollectFood waste drops into the OmniClear™ disposal tray.
  6. Sterilize360° UV bars eliminate bacteria and odors.
  7. VerifySensors confirm surfaces are spotless.
  8. DrySmart airflow finishes with a streak-free shine.
