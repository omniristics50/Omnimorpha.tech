Omnimorpha AI Dishwasher — Advanced Feature Suite

Intelligent cleaning, engineered for real homes and serious investors.

UV Sterilization System

Integrated 360° UV-bar arrays surround the wash chamber, eliminating up to 99.9% of bacteria and viruses after the final rinse for hospital-grade sanitation without harsh chemicals.

QuietShield™ Noise Suppression

A multi-layer acoustic shell, vibration control, and adaptive motor modulation reduce operating noise to under 38 dB—quieter than a whisper—while maintaining full cleaning performance.

Smart Detergent Injection

Precision dosing sensors analyze soil load, water hardness, and dish count before each cycle. The system injects exactly the right amount of detergent and rinse aid, cutting waste and improving clarity.

Transparent AI View Mode

The front panel becomes semi-transparent on command, overlaying AI mapping, brush motion, and jet paths in real time for a live “intelligence view.”

Water Recycling Option

A built-in micro-filtration module collects, purifies, and reuses up to 70% of rinse water for future cycles, reducing household consumption while maintaining strict hygienic standards.

Energy Savings Dashboard

A companion dashboard on the door and mobile app displays energy use, water savings, AI optimization rate, and cumulative eco-impact over time, turning each cycle into measurable value.
