Omnimorpha Regent X™ SUV Limousine

The Omnimorpha Regent X™ is an SUV-style limousine that merges luxury cabin design with off-road capability and tactical-ready options. It is built for leaders who need to move between city, highway, and off-grid environments without sacrificing comfort or security.

Platform Overview

Segment: SUV-style limousine · Focus: Tactical mobility + executive comfort · Energy: Graphene solid-state (vision) with AI-managed drive modes.

  • High ground clearance with adjustable suspension.
  • Full Omnimorpha AI Camera System™ coverage in urban and off-road conditions.
  • Halia-driven drive modes: City, Highway, Off-Road, and Tactical.

Standard Regent X™ Executive Edition

The base executive configuration focuses on comfort, presence, and smooth transitions between different environments.

  • Spacious rear lounge with executive seating and fold-out workspace.
  • Panoramic glass with climate-adaptive tint and anti-ice features.
  • Halia-managed “Executive Focus” and “Relaxed Transit” interior profiles.

Cabin & Experience

  • Omnimorpha crest integrated into seats and subtle interior details.
  • Noise-controlled cabin for calls, briefings, and negotiations.
  • VoiceLink Takeover™ for call and message routing while in motion.

Regent X™ Presidential Edition

The Presidential Edition adds armor and enhanced sensing layers, turning Regent X™ into a mission-ready SUV limousine for high-risk routes and regions.

Protection: Multi-layer body armor vision, ballistic glass, reinforced underbody · Sensing: Expanded AI Camera System zones + environmental scanning.

  • Discreet armored profile while maintaining Omnimorpha design language.
  • Secure comms hub synchronized with Halia for briefings and alerts.
  • Halia-monitored perimeter awareness, flagging unusual patterns (vision roadmap).

Regent X™ Tactical Off-Road Armor Variant

This variant leans into off-road, border, mountain, and remote region deployment.

  • Reinforced suspension, skid plates, and underbody protection.
  • Off-road tires and wheel systems tuned for snow, mud, and rock tracks.
  • Roof and side mounting points for mission equipment (non-weaponized vision).

Halia Tactical Layer

  • Route planning around terrain, weather, and known risk regions.
  • Energy budgeting for long-range off-grid missions.
  • Live system health reports for fleet operators.

Elite Investor & Limited Editions

Regent X™ can also be configured as an Elite Investor Edition, mirroring some of the ultra-lux elements of the Imperial Limousine™ while keeping the SUV stance.

  • Unique paint finishes and interior palettes exclusive to early backers.
  • Special Halia themes, sounds, and cabin animations for Regent X™ owners.
  • Numbered units for collectors and flagship partners.
