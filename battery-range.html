Battery Architecture

The Omnimorpha Graphene Solid-State Battery™ is a high-voltage platform using graphene-enhanced solid electrolytes, multi-layer pouch cells, and an integrated thermal management spine. It is designed to support extreme performance vehicles while maintaining daily-driver efficiency and fleet reliability.

  • Pack voltage: 800–900 V class architecture
  • Energy options: 100 kWh · 130 kWh · 160 kWh pack families
  • Chemistry: Graphene-reinforced solid-state lithium system
  • Cooling: Embedded liquid channels + phase-change thermal plates
  • Control layer: Halia-driven Battery Intelligence (BMS + predictive analytics)

Fast Charging Profile

The Omnimorpha battery platform is built for high-power DC fast charging and future OmniCharge Field™ energy systems, reducing downtime for both private owners and fleets.

  • DC fast charge (public network): 10–80% in ~12–15 minutes (350 kW class target)
  • Peak charge power: Up to 420 kW on supported infrastructure (concept target)
  • AC home / depot: 11–22 kW bi-directional ready (V2H / V2G capable)
  • Thermal-aware curves: Halia automatically adjusts charge rate based on pack temperature and health

For future OmniCharge Field™ deployments, the same pack architecture can be tuned for over-the-air charging concepts and inductive pads, aligning vehicle energy with the broader Omnimorpha ecosystem.

Safety & Thermal Management

Safety is treated as a first-class design constraint. The Graphene Solid-State Battery™ uses multiple hardware and software layers to reduce risk and respond proactively to abnormal conditions.

  • Solid-state core: Reduced risk of thermal runaway vs. conventional liquid-electrolyte cells
  • Graphene heat spreaders: Rapid distribution of heat away from local hot spots
  • Multi-zone cooling: Independent loops for high-load modules and sensitive electronics
  • AI anomaly detection: Halia monitors temperature, voltage, and resistance signatures in real time
  • Crash isolation: Automatic high-voltage disconnect in the event of impact or structural intrusion

Degradation & Lifecycle

Omnimorpha’s battery platform is engineered for long-term use, including high-mileage fleet vehicles. The focus is on maintaining usable range well past standard ownership cycles.

  • Cycle life target: 3,000+ full cycles before major capacity loss (concept target)
  • Projected lifespan: 300,000–400,000+ miles (482,000–643,000+ km) under normal use
  • Halia Health Score: Owner-visible battery health and projected end-of-life range
  • Second-life pathway: Pack design suitable for stationary storage or microgrid deployment
