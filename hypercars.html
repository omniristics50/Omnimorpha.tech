Omnimorpha Hypercars

Omnimorpha Hypercars bring together graphene solid-state energy, DriveCore™ electric performance, Halia AI, and a full mirrorless AI camera system. Hyper-V, Hyper-X 2.0, and Hyper-V Ultima are designed as the halo vehicles of the Omnimorpha ecosystem.

Lineup Overview

The Omnimorpha hypercar family is built around a single vision: show what Halia and Omnimorpha can do at the absolute edge of performance and design.

  • Hyper-V — Grand tourer flagship.
  • Hyper-X 2.0 — Track-focused hypercar.
  • Hyper-V Ultima — Investor and limited-series edition.

Omnimorpha Hyper-V — Grand Tourer Flagship

Hyper-V is the grand touring hypercar for long-range, high-speed travel with Halia quietly managing everything in the background — from climate and vision to charging, navigation, and in-cabin experience.

Platform: 2+2 grand tourer · Energy: Graphene solid-state pack ~120 kWh (target) · Range: ~350 mi mixed (target) · Performance: 0–60 mph ~2.1 s (target), AWD torque vectoring · Control: DriveCore-V™ performance controller.

  • Mirrorless cabin powered by Omnimorpha AI Camera System™ (no physical mirrors).
  • Full Halia integration for route planning, adaptive suspension profiles, and driver coaching.
  • Graphene-enhanced solid-state pack for long life, fast charging, and thermal stability.

Omnimorpha Hyper-X 2.0 — Track-Focused Hypercar

Hyper-X 2.0 is the sharper, more aggressive sibling — designed around track performance, aero efficiency, and data-heavy analysis through Halia.

Platform: Two-seat low-slung hypercar · Energy: Graphene solid-state pack ~100 kWh (target) · Performance: 0–60 mph ~1.9 s (target), high-downforce aero package · Control: DriveCore-X™ track controller + AI stability layer.

  • Live lap timing, sector analysis, and tire/temperature monitoring via Halia.
  • Aero surfaces tuned for both high-speed stability and cornering downforce.
  • AI-assisted modes for “Learning Laps” and “Attack Laps” with driver feedback afterwards.

Omnimorpha Hyper-V Ultima — Investor Edition

Hyper-V Ultima represents a halo configuration of the Hyper-V platform — built as an ultra-limited series concept for investors and top partners.

Edition: Limited, numbered series (concept positioning) · Focus: Maximum luxury + showcase of Omnimorpha technology · Experience: Halia-curated interior profiles, bespoke materials, and exclusive modes.

  • Unique interior design themes, trim materials, and lighting not available on standard Hyper-V.
  • Exclusive Halia “Ultima Mode” profile for sound, lighting, and performance feel.
  • Positioned as a collector-grade asset that also acts as a rolling tech demonstrator.

Core Technologies in Omnimorpha Hypercars

All Omnimorpha hypercars are designed as technology showcases — spotlighting systems that will cascade down into the rest of the Omnimorpha vehicle lineup.

DriveCore™ Electric Performance Engine

DriveCore™ is Omnimorpha’s central electric performance controller, balancing power delivery, efficiency, and battery health.

  • Real-time torque vectoring and traction management for each axle.
  • Adaptive power curves for Comfort, Sport, Track, and Custom modes.
  • Continuous monitoring of thermal loads to protect the graphene solid-state pack.

Graphene Solid-State Energy System

Hyper-V and Hyper-X 2.0 are built around a graphene-enhanced solid-state battery vision. This aligns with Omnimorpha’s long-term move from traditional lithium-ion toward more stable, safer, and higher-density energy storage.

  • Higher energy density vs. conventional packs (target positioning).
  • Improved safety characteristics and thermal stability.
  • Potentially faster charge cycles and longer lifespan per pack.

Omnimorpha AI Camera System™ (Mirrorless Vision)

All hypercars use the Omnimorpha AI Camera System™ to replace traditional mirrors and provide 360° awareness.

  • Ultra-wide AI cameras replace side and rear mirrors, eliminating blind spots.
  • LiDAR + AI Vision for object detection, lane boundaries, and environmental awareness.
  • Facial and body recognition for secure, keyless and lockless vehicle entry.

Halia Integration & VoiceLink Takeover™

Halia sits at the center of the hypercar experience — not just as an assistant, but as the orchestrator of drive modes, comfort, communication, and safety.

  • Halia VoiceLink Takeover™ syncs the driver’s phone on entry and routes calls/texts through voice only.
  • Drive summaries after each trip: energy use, performance stats, and safety events.
  • Predictive suggestions: when to charge, when to take a break, and where to push or relax.

Climate Adaptation System

Omnimorpha’s climate adaptation system dynamically manages exterior and interior temperatures for performance, comfort, and safety.

  • Snow/ice melt functions on critical surfaces during winter conditions.
  • Heat management in extreme sun to protect occupants and batteries.
  • Adaptive visibility control for glass and camera elements in rain or fog.

Investor Positioning

Omnimorpha Hypercars are not intended as mass-volume vehicles. They act as:

  • Halo products that define Omnimorpha’s design, energy, and AI capabilities.
  • Testbeds for technologies that later deploy into regular vehicles and snow platforms.
  • High-impact brand assets for media, partnerships, and flagship investors.

For investors, the hypercar program is a strategic pillar that amplifies the entire ecosystem — from AI cameras and DriveCore™ to graphene solid-state research, Halia, and future Omnimorpha vehicle lines.
