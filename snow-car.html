Overview

Designed for cities and residential zones buried by winter storms, the Snow Car™ combines electric all-wheel drive, AI terrain sensing, and a built-in snow-melting system that turns snow into managed ice blocks instead of messy plowed piles.

Use Case: Daily winter commuter, city fleets, emergency access · Drive: Electric AWD with Halia traction control · System: OISM integrated underbody intake + melt channels.

Omnimorpha Intelligent Snow-Melting System (OISM)

OISM is the signature technology that makes the Snow Car™ different from any conventional snow vehicle. Instead of pushing snow aside, it intelligently collects, melts, compresses, and stores it.

How OISM Works in the Snow Car™

  1. Intake: Front and underbody intake ducts pull snow into the system as the vehicle moves.
  2. Melting Chamber: Electric heating elements and waste-heat from the powertrain help melt the snow.
  3. Filtration: Grit and debris are filtered out for clean melting and smoother operation.
  4. Compression: Melted water is directed into a compression cell that forms solid ice blocks.
  5. Storage: Ice blocks are stacked in a rear or underfloor storage bay with modular capacity.
  6. Smart Disposal: Halia suggests safe disposal zones or re-purposes the ice for controlled release.

All stages are monitored in real time by Halia, balancing energy usage, traction, and storage limits.

Design & Performance

The Snow Car™ is compact, stable, and built to feel like a “normal car” with extraordinary winter capability.

Platform: Compact 4-door or 2+2 hatchback (concept positioning) · Energy: Graphene-enhanced solid-state pack (future vision) · Traction: Halia-controlled torque vectoring for snow and ice.

  • Low center of gravity for confidence on icy roads.
  • Camera + sensor fusion for lane-keeping even when road markings are covered.
  • Climate Adaptation System gently warms body surfaces to reduce ice buildup.

Halia Integration

  • Monitors snow depth, intake rate, and storage usage in real time.
  • Suggests optimal routes and driving speeds based on storm conditions.
  • Provides driver coaching for safer braking and cornering on ice.

Investor Positioning

The Omnimorpha Snow Car™ addresses a clear, under-served need in winter-dominant regions: personal vehicles that can continue operating even under heavy snowfall without relying on external plowing.

  • Target customers: northern cities, municipalities, utility fleets, and private owners.
  • Expandable platform: same OISM logic can be scaled to larger vehicles.
  • Brand impact: showcases Omnimorpha as a company that solves real climate challenges.
