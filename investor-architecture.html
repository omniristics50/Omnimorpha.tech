NEXUS — Investor Architecture

NEXUS is a software-only, read-only intelligence layer. It simulates a battery digital twin, predicts risk with validation, generates explainable strategy recommendations, and streams live state to the web dashboard.

1) Signal

Digital Twin Telemetry

Simulated SOC, temperature, trend, impedance proxy, voltage/current, cycle count.

2) Interpret

Risk Model + Validation

Risk score + tier with confidence. Validation dampens transient spikes to reduce false alarms.

3) Explain

Narrative Reasoning

Human-readable explanation: what changed, why risk shifted, and what drivers mattered.

4) Recommend

Strategy Engine

Ranked mitigation strategies. Read-only recommendations; no execution layer.

5) Remember

Timeline + Memory

Event stream: state transitions, strategy updates, and narrative snapshots over time.

6) Govern

Audit + Safety

Read-only mode, versioning, traceable outputs. Designed to be investor-safe and demo-safe.

Read-only guarantee

The NEXUS demo does not control hardware or execute actions. It simulates state, predicts risk, and recommends strategies for interpretation. This reduces operational risk and keeps the demo investor-safe.
