Advanced Features for a Smarter Home

360° UV Sterilization

Side-mounted UV bars and internal 360° UV-bar arrays eliminate up to 99.9% of bacteria and viruses, delivering hospital-grade sanitation after every cycle without harsh chemicals.

QuietShield™ Noise Suppression

A multi-layer acoustic shell, vibration control, and adaptive motor modulation keep operating noise under 38 dB—quieter than a whisper—while maintaining full cleaning performance.

Smart Detergent Injection

Precision dosing sensors analyze soil load, water hardness, and dish count before each cycle. The dishwasher injects the exact detergent and rinse-aid dose every time, cutting waste and improving clarity.

AI Transparent View Mode

On the command “Halia, show me inside”, the front panel becomes semi-transparent, revealing live AI mapping lines, brush motion, and jet paths for a real-time intelligence view.

Water Recycling Module

A micro-filtration module purifies and reuses up to 70% of rinse water for the next cycle, reducing household water consumption while preserving strict hygiene standards.

Energy Savings Dashboard

A companion dashboard on the door and in the mobile app tracks energy and water usage, efficiency gains, AI optimization rate, and long-term eco-savings, turning each cycle into measurable value for users and investors.
