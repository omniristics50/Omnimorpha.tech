CryoCore™ AI Cooling Engine

The Omnimorpha IntelliFridge™ uses the next-generation CryoCore™ engine built with dual-stage AI compressors and graphene thermal plates. Cooling adjusts intelligently based on shelf activity, food volume, and temperature history.

  • Graphene Thermal Plate: Ultra-fast cooling with 30% lower energy use.
  • Dual-Stage Cryo Compressors: Whisper-quiet and AI regulated.
  • OmniFlow Air Channels: Hidden airflow for a seamless design.
  • Frost Prediction Logic: Prevents frost before it forms.

Halia Vision Cameras & Intelligent Sensors

The IntelliFridge™ uses AI-enhanced cameras and sensors to track inventory, expiration timelines, and food quality automatically.

  • Top Vision Camera: Recognizes items, packaging, and freshness.
  • Shelf Cameras: Detect added or removed items instantly.
  • Infrared Sensors: Monitors food temperature for safety.
  • Shelf Weight Sensors: Tracks quantity changes.
  • Air Quality Sensors: Predicts ripeness through ethylene detection.

AI-Adaptive Shelving System

The interior automatically adapts to your lifestyle. Shelves reposition based on the height and size of stored items.

  • Self-Adjusting Shelves: Motorized rails adapt automatically.
  • Multi-Zone Climate Control: Independent shelf cooling.
  • Produce Preservation Drawers: AI humidity-managed zones.
  • Gold Ambient Illumination: Premium Omnimorpha lighting.

HaliaSync™ Smart Integration

Halia transforms the IntelliFridge™ into a fully intelligent kitchen ecosystem.

  • Automatic Inventory Tracking
  • AI Shopping Lists synced to devices
  • Meal Recommendations based on stored items
  • Expiration Alerts before food spoils
