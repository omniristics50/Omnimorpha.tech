Omnimorpha Engine Architecture

The Omnimorpha power platform evolves across three generations — from the original concept Core Engine, through the hybrid-realistic V1.5, to the production-ready Circuit Engine (V2). This page presents the visual and material breakdown of each stage.

Engine Evolution — V1 → V1.5 → V2

Each generation refines the balance between design language, material science, and manufacturable power architecture. V1 defines the visual DNA. V1.5 upgrades that design into a realistic, graphene-based hybrid system. V2 becomes the investor-ready, industrial-grade engine platform for future Omnimorpha vehicles.

V1 — Omnimorpha Core Engine Concept

  • Conceptual hybrid of mechanical drive and AI-driven visual identity.
  • Reinforced polymer and aluminum structural frame.
  • Electroluminescent circuit housing defining Omnimorpha’s visual DNA.
  • Designed as the origin point for future engine architecture.

V1.5 — Core Engine V1.5 Hybrid Realistic

  • Graphene solid-state power block integrated into the original V1 design.
  • Aluminum + CFRP chassis with copper microchannel cooling.
  • Kevlar-reinforced high-voltage belt system for realistic energy routing.
  • Dedicated AI control module for intelligent power and thermal management.

V2 — Circuit Engine™ Investor Edition

  • Graphene solid-state cell stack with multi-layer high-voltage architecture.
  • CNC-machined aluminum housing with gold-plated HV busbars and terminals.
  • Dedicated AI control slab for Halia-driven diagnostics and power orchestration.
  • Built as a scalable, production-ready platform for Omnimorpha vehicles.

How the PowerCore™ & Circuit Engine™ Work Together

The PowerCore™ and Circuit Engine™ form a unified propulsion ecosystem engineered for instant energy transfer, intelligent cooling, and unmatched efficiency. This section explains how the two systems function together inside the hood of an Omnimorpha vehicle.

Power Initialization
The process begins inside the PowerCore™, where graphene solid-state layers are scanned in real time. The AI evaluates voltage, thermal load, and stored capacity before releasing energy.

Energy Mapping & Routing
An AI-driven power module calculates the precise route energy must take to reach the Circuit Engine™. This ensures optimized torque, acceleration, and minimal energy loss.

Engine Activation
The Circuit Engine™ receives the energy through gold-plated conductors and multilayer pathways. These activate the engine’s power matrix, enabling instant and controlled output.

Thermal Management
A shared liquid-cooling microchannel system stabilizes both the PowerCore™ and the engine simultaneously. This maintains efficiency even during high-temperature or high-performance driving.

Unified Operation
As the vehicle moves, the AI continuously adjusts energy flow, cooling rates, and performance demand. Every component communicates in real time to maintain balance, power, and safety.

Continuous Intelligent Flow
Regenerative braking returns energy to the PowerCore™, completing the loop and ensuring long-term efficiency and extended battery lifespan.
