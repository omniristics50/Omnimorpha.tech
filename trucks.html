Truck Lineup

Snow Truck™

Designed to melt and collect snow while driving. The Snow Truck compresses and stores ice using the Omnimorpha Ice Management System (OISM™), ensuring smooth travel, real-time adaptability, and zero clogging.

Cargo Truck™

Built for intelligent logistics, this AI-guided truck adjusts hauling modes based on weight, terrain, and traffic. Interior Halia sensors manage suspension, battery optimization, and smart freight loading/unloading systems.

Multi-Terrain Truck™

For extreme conditions — sand, ice, rain, or rock. This truck uses AI-tuned grip control, dynamic balance shifting, and magnetic drive locks for rock-crawl and slope defense modes. Powered by Omnimorpha's DriveCore AI engine.
