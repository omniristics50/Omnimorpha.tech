Advanced Features for a Smarter Home

360° UV Sterilization

Side-mounted UV bars eliminate bacteria and odors using medical-grade sanitation after every cycle, delivering chemical-free hygiene and long-term freshness.

QuietShield™ Noise Suppression

Operates under 38 dB with a multi-layer acoustic shell, vibration control, and adaptive motor balancing—whisper-quiet cycles in any kitchen.

Smart Detergent Injection

AI calculates soil level, water hardness, and dish count to inject the exact detergent and rinse-aid dose every time, cutting waste and improving clarity.

AI Transparent View Mode

On the command “Halia, show me inside”, the front panel becomes semi-transparent, revealing live AI mapping lines, brush motion, and jet paths.

Water Recycling Module

A micro-filtration module purifies and reuses up to 70% of rinse water for the next cycle, reducing household consumption while maintaining strict hygienic standards.

Energy Savings Dashboard

Tracks water and electricity usage, efficiency gains, and long-term eco-savings in the app and on-door display, turning every cycle into measurable value.

Why This Is Revolutionary

The Omnimorpha AI Dishwasher replaces fixed, pre-set wash cycles with a fully adaptive cleaning engine. On every load, AI recognition cameras and sensors scan each item, build a 3D map of the rack, and dynamically adjust brush torque, jet pressure, water volume, and temperature. Instead of treating dishes as a batch, the system treats every item as a unique cleaning profile.

For investors, this is a differentiated, defensible platform: a software-defined appliance that becomes smarter over time, locking in users and opening a path for subscription analytics, remote diagnostics, and eventually fleet-level deployments in hospitality and commercial kitchens.
