Architecture | Graphene Solid-State Core

The Circuit Engine™ is built around a solid-state power spine that routes energy from Omni Battery™ into motors, auxiliary systems, and future add-ons — all orchestrated through a single AI control brain.

  • Graphene-enhanced power modules arranged in a compact, serviceable layout.
  • Multi-channel bus architecture for traction, suspension, climate, and auxiliary loads.
  • Dedicated performance zones for launch torque, cruising efficiency, and emergency reserve.
  • Native integration with Omni Battery™ pack logic for health, range, and lifecycle optimization.

AI Control | Halia-Linked Drive Brain

Instead of a traditional ECU cluster, the Circuit Engine™ uses an AI-linked control brain that talks directly with Halia, the Omni Battery™, and the vehicle’s sensor suite to constantly rebalance power and safety.

  • Real-time torque vectoring and power distribution across motors and axles.
  • Continuous health checks on every module, sensing anomalies before they become failures.
  • Dynamic maps for temperature, load, and vibration across the entire engine spine.
  • Over-the-air firmware and AI model updates for performance, safety, and efficiency upgrades.

How Circuit Engine™ Differs From a Typical EV Platform

Where most EVs treat batteries, inverters, motors, and cooling as loosely connected modules, the Circuit Engine™ treats them as one intelligent organism — with every part aware of the others in real time.

Typical EV Architecture

  • Separate suppliers for packs, inverters, and cooling systems.
  • Limited cross-talk between subsystems; updates often siloed.
  • Thermal management optimized per component, not as a unified spine.
  • Range, performance, and safety tuned in trade-off, not in harmony.

Omnimorpha Circuit Engine™

  • Single Omnimorpha-owned architecture from Omni Battery™ to drive motors.
  • AI brain continuously rebalancing power, temperature, and lifecycle stress.
  • Graphene-enhanced cooling and solid-state control for high stability.
  • Built as core IP that can be licensed or kept exclusive to Omnimorpha vehicles.
