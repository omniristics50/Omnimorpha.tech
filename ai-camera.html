The Problem: Outdated Vehicle Vision

Vehicles are getting smarter, but their vision systems are still built on a century-old foundation of mirrors, fragmented sensors, and basic cameras.

  • Blind spots and limited visibility in fog, snow, smoke, and at night.
  • Separate cameras for parking, lane assist, and driver monitoring.
  • Rising vehicle theft with no intelligent prediction or real-time evidence.
  • No full-body biometric access — keys and fobs are still the norm.
  • Accidents often lack reliable, tamper-proof video records.

The Omnimorpha Solution

The Omnimorpha AI Camera System™ replaces fragmented legacy hardware with a single, unified vision platform that sees, understands, and protects.

  • Mirrorless 360° camera visibility with LiDAR + AI fusion.
  • OmniGlass™ biometric entry using face and full-body recognition.
  • Environmental Penetration Vision (EPV™) for fog, snow, smoke, and darkness.
  • OmniEvidence™ encrypted recording for legal-grade incident evidence.
  • Predictive anti-theft intelligence and tow detection.
  • NeuralVision™ smart city integration for fleets and autonomous networks.
