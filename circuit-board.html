Omnimorpha Circuit Board Engine™

The world’s first AI-powered vehicle circuit system—eliminating traditional mechanical engines through high-performance electric circuitry, graphene energy transfer, and software-regulated drive control.

Technical Specifications

  • Power Source: Graphene Solid-State Battery
  • Board Material: Carbon-Fiber Reinforced Polymer with Nano-Layered Graphene
  • Cooling System: AI-Regulated Microfluidic Channels + Smart Thermal Conduction
  • AI Core: HALIA DriveOS Embedded Microcontroller
  • Voltage Management: Intelligent Circuit Switching (ICS)
  • Diagnostics: Self-Healing & Real-Time Circuit Monitoring
  • Motion Control: Magnetic Linear Acceleration Grid (MLAG)
  • Footprint: 30% smaller than traditional EV powertrain

How It Works

1. Power Injection: The graphene battery sends ultra-fast electrical pulses directly into the Omnimorpha Circuit Board Engine.

2. AI-Directed Routing: Halia AI distributes power across all drive systems via intelligent circuit pathways, ensuring efficient torque and energy delivery.

3. Real-Time Control: The system continuously adjusts performance using real-time driver behavior, terrain, and environmental data.

4. Thermal Balance: Advanced microfluidics carry heat away through nano-channels, stabilized by AI-controlled conductive zones to prevent overheating.

5. Self-Diagnosis: Every node on the board is self-monitoring, with instant failover if anomalies are detected—minimizing downtime and eliminating traditional diagnostics.
