VELOCIÉ™ Electric Motorcycles

VELOCIÉ™ is Omnimorpha’s electric motorcycle division — a lineup built around Halia-linked intelligence, DriveCore™ electric powertrains, and a smart helmet HUD system designed for riders who live between performance and precision.

Lineup Overview

The VELOCIÉ™ range covers flagship street performance, track-only racing, daily commuting, long-distance touring, and stealth night riding — all connected to Halia through the VELOCIÉ Smart Helmet system.

  • L-X — Flagship street model
  • L-XR — Track-focused racing edition
  • L-E — Compact city commuter
  • LXV — Two-seat touring
  • L-NIGHT — Stealth night-riding model
  • Smart Helmet — Halia-linked HUD & telemetry

VELOCIÉ L-X — Flagship

The VELOCIÉ L-X is the flagship street motorcycle for riders who want instant torque, clean design, and Halia in their visor on every ride.

Powertrain: DriveCore™ ~180 hp equivalent · Battery: Graphene solid-state ~16 kWh · Range: ~180 mi mixed (target) · Interface: HaliaLink™ helmet HUD + live telemetry.

  • Balanced for daily performance and weekend canyon runs.
  • Instant torque delivery with precise traction control.
  • Halia provides route suggestions, safety alerts, and ride summaries.

VELOCIÉ L-XR — Racing Edition

The VELOCIÉ L-XR takes the L-X platform and pushes it into track territory, tuned for lap times, consistency, and coaching feedback through Halia.

Powertrain: DriveCore-R™ ~200 hp equivalent (track tune) · Battery: High-discharge performance pack · 0–60 mph: ~2.5 s (target) · Interface: HaliaLink™ lap timing & coach mode.

  • Track-tuned power curve and high-grip chassis setup.
  • Halia records lap times, braking zones, and corner speeds.
  • Coach mode provides post-session feedback and suggested improvements.

VELOCIÉ L-E — Commuter

The VELOCIÉ L-E is built for the city — agile, efficient, and easy to park, with enough range for a full week of urban commuting on a single charge.

Powertrain: DriveCore-E™ ~95 hp equivalent · Battery: ~12 kWh · Range: ~150 mi city (target) · Charging: DC fast charge 20–80% in ~22 minutes.

  • Compact footprint for tight streets and dense parking.
  • Halia Vision Assist for blind-spot and traffic awareness.
  • Optimized for low running costs and daily reliability.

VELOCIÉ LXV — Two-Seat Touring

The VELOCIÉ LXV is designed for long-distance travel, comfort, and two-up riding — pairing electric performance with touring practicality.

Powertrain: DriveCore-T™ ~150 hp equivalent · Battery: ~20 kWh · Range: ~240 mi highway (target) · Chassis: Adaptive suspension + integrated storage.

  • Comfort-focused ergonomics for rider and passenger.
  • Dynamic suspension adapts to load, road quality, and speed.
  • Side and rear storage integrated cleanly into the bodywork.

VELOCIÉ L-NIGHT — Stealth

The VELOCIÉ L-NIGHT is the stealth-focused model, built for night rides, low-visibility conditions, and riders who want a more subtle presence.

Powertrain: DriveCore-N™ ~170 hp equivalent · Lighting: Adaptive low-glare lighting · Vision: NightSense™ (IR + low-light AI) · Range: ~170 mi mixed (target).

  • Infrared and low-light cameras support NightSense™ visibility.
  • Lighting profile reduces glare for oncoming traffic.
  • Halia overlays navigation and hazard alerts onto the HUD at night.

Special Variants & Alt Editions

VELOCIÉ Alt editions are special trims of the L-X and L-XR platforms, designed for limited runs, collaborations, or investor-exclusive series.

VELOCIÉ L-X Alt — Flagship Special

Cosmetic and tuning upgrades based on the L-X street model. May include unique colorways, limited interior/seat materials, and special Halia themes.

VELOCIÉ L-XR Alt — Track Special

Limited-run L-XR variant with track-focused aero, unique liveries, and additional telemetry views for advanced riders and racing partners.

VELOCIÉ Smart Helmet & HaliaLink™

At the center of the VELOCIÉ experience is the Smart Helmet, which connects each motorcycle to Halia through a head-up display and live data link.

  • HUD: Speed, range, navigation, and key alerts projected in the rider’s view.
  • Telemetry: Real-time data from the bike: lean angles, power usage, temps, and more.
  • Voice: Hands-free communication with Halia for routes, calls, and quick commands.
  • Safety: Crash detection, emergency contact alerts, and ride logging.

For investors, the Smart Helmet represents not just safety and UX, but also a recurring revenue opportunity via hardware upgrades, in-app services, and data-aware features.
